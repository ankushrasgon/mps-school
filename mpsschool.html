Welcome @MPS

Modern Public School was established in 1971 by Modern Public School Education Society (Regd.) with the avowed object of serving the cause of education in the NCT of Delhi, particularly in Shalimar Bagh and neighbouring areas. It had a modest beginning in a residential building in Ashok Vihar and it is heartening to note that, the school has a successful track record in imparting education since 1971.
